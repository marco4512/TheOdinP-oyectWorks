Zucchini Pasta

Pasta with zucchini in a garlicky sauce. Serve with crusty bread and a chilled white wine, if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound zucchini
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup parsley
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 1/2 cup pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Cut zucchini into bite-size pieces.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together olive oil, butter, flour, milk, water, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add zucchini and mix well.
  5. Form a ball with your hands.
  6. Pour the mixture into a 9-inch round baking dish.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes.
